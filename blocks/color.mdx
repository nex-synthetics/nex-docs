In this section, we will go over how to use your own uploaded base image asset to control your  image generation. 

<Tip>
Upload **images** that are as **high res** as possible and have the least amount of blurriness.
</Tip>

#### 1. Hover over the image that you have uploaded for Pose Control 

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/blocks/color/1.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

#### 2. Click on “Use As” 

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/blocks/color/2.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

#### 3. select "Color Image”

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/blocks/color/3.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

#### 4. Head back to Prompt on the left action bar

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/blocks/color/4.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

#### 5. Type in your prompt and click “Generate” below or press “Enter” on your keyboard.
