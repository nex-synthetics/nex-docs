NEX has three subscription tiers. Pay month-to-month or for the entire year for a discount of \$5/month for the Professional plan and \$10/month for the Organization plan. Each subscription plan includes access to all NEX features, a maximum of 8 concurrent generations, private generations, increased asset storage, and more.

### Subscribe to a Plan
1. Visit the NEX Console

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/pricing/2.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

2. Click on “Upgrade” in the top right of the left action bar

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/pricing/3.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

3. On the Plans page (https://console.nex.art/settings/plans), pick the plan that applies to you.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/pricing/4.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

### Payment Methods

Payments through Cash App Pay, Bank, or others [supported by Stripe](https://stripe.com/docs/payments/cards/supported-card-brands) are currently accepted: credit or debit cards issued by services like Mastercard, VISA, or American Express. 

PayPal is not supported

### Plan Management

View and manage your plan at: 

https://console.nex.art/settings/workspace?page=billing

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/pricing/5.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>


### Cancellation

Head to https://console.nex.art/settings/workspace?page=billing to cancel a subscription at any time. 

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/pricing/6.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

Cancellations are effective immediately. 

### Switch Plans

When you’re ready to bring your organization onto NEX, you may choose to upgrade immediately  at https://console.nex.art/settings/plans. If you are already on the Professional Plan, then you will only be charged the difference in price.
