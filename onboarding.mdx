### To start: 


#### 1. Click either of the “Try for Free” buttons 

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started/landing-page.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

#### 2. Fill in the required boxes of the Sign Up module and click “Continue” at the bottom.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started/2.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

#### 3. After deciding what you’re planning to use NEX for, select the option and click “Get Started”. Ensure that the option you want is the one outlined in green.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started/5.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

#### 4. Once you’re in, you’ll be presented with a tutorial modal. If it’s your first time using NEX, we encourage you to watch the tutorial. Alternatively, you can exit out of the modal by clicking the X in the top right corner; or you can head straight to the Artboard by clicking “Start Generating” at the bottom of the modal. 

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started/4.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>
